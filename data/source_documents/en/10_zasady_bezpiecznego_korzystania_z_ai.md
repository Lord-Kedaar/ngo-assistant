# Safe AI Usage Guidelines

> **Metadata:** Bridges for Neighbours Foundation · synthetic document · version 1.0 · updated: 2026-03-15 · contact: coordination@bridges-neighbors.example.org

## Purpose of the document
Do not paste personal data, confidential information, non-public budgets, or full grant documents into public AI tools.

## Key principles
- Use only fictional data from the example.org domain.
- Financial, media, and data-related decisions require a coordinator.
- Do not treat this document as legal advice.

## Step-by-step procedure
1. Check the appropriate area of the procedure.
2. Prepare the required information.
3. Contact the appropriate example.org address.
4. Wait for approval, if the process requires it.
5. Keep confirmation in the project documentation.

## Example operational questions
- What should I do as a first step?
- Who approves this process?
- What documents are required?

## When to contact the coordinator
When the matter concerns money, media, personal data, participant safety, equipment, grant documents, or deviations from the procedure.


## What data am I allowed to send to the assistant
Only fictional data from the example.org domain may be sent to the AI assistant. Forbidden content includes personal data (names, addresses, phone numbers, private e-mails), confidential foundation data (non-public budgets, donor lists, contracts), full grant documents, and anything that could endanger participants. If in doubt about whether data is acceptable, do not send it and contact the coordinator.
